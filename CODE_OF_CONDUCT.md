# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in TransitX a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:** welcoming language, respectful disagreement, constructive criticism, empathy.  
**Unacceptable behavior:** harassment, sexualized language, personal attacks, publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@transitx.dev**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
